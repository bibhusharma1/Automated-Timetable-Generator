How Attendance Data is Used

Attendance data helps the system identify optimal time slots for different subjects. The machine learning algorithm uses this information to:

  • Schedule popular subjects during high-attendance time slots
  • Identify patterns in student engagement across different times of day
  • Optimize classroom utilization based on expected attendance
  • Balance the timetable to maximize overall student participation
